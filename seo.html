Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between a flat fee and AUM financial advisor?

An AUM (assets under management) advisor charges a percentage of your portfolio — typically 0.5%–1.5% annually. On a $1M portfolio, that's $5,000–$15,000 per year, every year, whether you got good advice or not. A flat fee advisor like Impact Wealth Partners charges a set fee regardless of portfolio size, making comprehensive financial planning accessible and transparent.
